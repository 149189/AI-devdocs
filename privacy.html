AI DevDocs is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains how we handle information when you use our site.

Information We Collect

AI DevDocs is a static website that operates entirely in your browser. We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal information to external servers.

  • No accounts or registration - You can use the site without creating an account.
  • No server-side tracking - We do not use server-side analytics or tracking pixels.
  • Local analytics only - We store anonymous usage statistics (topic views, search terms) in your browser's localStorage. This data never leaves your device.

Cookies and Local Storage

We use your browser's localStorage for two purposes:

  • Theme preference - Remembers your light/dark mode choice.
  • Usage analytics - Tracks which topics you view and what you search for. This data stays on your device and is only visible in the admin dashboard on the same browser.

You can clear this data at any time through your browser's settings or the admin dashboard.

Third-Party Services

AI DevDocs uses the following third-party CDN services to load libraries:

  • Bootstrap (via jsDelivr CDN) - For layout and styling
  • Marked.js (via jsDelivr CDN) - For Markdown rendering

In the future, we may integrate Google AdSense for advertising. If activated, Google may use cookies to serve ads based on your browsing history. You can opt out of personalized advertising at Google Ads Settings.

Data Security

Since all data remains in your browser's localStorage, it is protected by your browser's security model. We do not have access to this data.

Children's Privacy

AI DevDocs is an educational resource suitable for all ages. We do not knowingly collect any personal information from anyone, including children.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Changes will be reflected on this page with an updated date.

Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy, please visit our Contact page.
